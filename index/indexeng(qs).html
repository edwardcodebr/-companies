Who We Are

  • IPP is a civil society organization without economic purposes (OSCIP), whose main objective is the development of studies and research in various fields of human knowledge, the prospecting of alternative and innovative technologies, as well as developing work in co-execution with other related institutions, thus anticipating potential demands of the Brazilian and global industry.
