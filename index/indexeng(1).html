Mission

Produce and stimulate research, development, and high-performance innovations based on evolutionary development paradigms, by Brazilian companies, to maintain and generate businesses with a high degree of competitiveness in distributed, intelligent, and integrated systems of 4.0 environments.

Vision

To be a local, national, and international reference in research, development, and innovation of evolutionary, cyber-physical, and sustainable environments, to generate partnerships between academia, government, and companies, anticipating potential demands of Brazilian and global industries.

Values

  • Respect for life and human dignity
  • Commitment to scientific studies
  • Appreciation of people and their skills
  • Quality and excellence in scientific work
