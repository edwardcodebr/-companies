Research Lines

  • Energy Efficiency, Photovoltaic Solar Energy Systems, Materials and Compounds Analysis, Calibration and Measurement Analysis of Electrical, Mechanical, and General Physical Quantities, Refrigeration Systems Analysis and Evaluation, Development of Systems with Power Electronics, 4.0 Environments Systems (Industry 4.0), Internet of Things (IoT)

Intelligent System for Skin Cancer Treatment Analysis and Diagnosis

  • Developing an integrated system with software and artificial intelligence resources to analyze and provide diagnosis for the most suitable treatment for skin cancer.

    Benefits:
    The project will benefit the low-income population in the diagnosis and prevention of cancer treatment and its consequences.
    Laboratory with equipment infrastructure for cancer analysis and diagnosis.
    Computational platform for issuing skin cancer diagnosis.
    Professional training in artificial intelligence.

Sistema inteligente para detectar Malformações e defeitos congênitos no feto.

  • The system consists of using artificial intelligence algorithms in the context of ultrasound. The idea is to detect fetal malformations and issues in the female reproductive system using these algorithms.

    Benefits:
    The project will benefit the population through early treatment of potential diseases in both normal and high-risk pregnancies.
    Laboratory equipped with infrastructure for cancer analysis and diagnosis.
    Computational platform for issuing skin cancer diagnoses.
    Training professionals in artificial intelligence.

Smart App using Artificial Intelligence to support Covid-19 monitoring in different geographic regions, providing healthcare coverage in major urban centers.

  • Develop a smart application that will collect and map the population's situation regarding the disease (COVID-19), providing statistical data projections necessary for interpreting the epidemiological scenario.

    Benefits:
    Establish information that will be part of the application development;
    Indicate possible COVID-19 situations reported by users;
    Track individuals who have contracted COVID-19;
    Promote a history of recovered individuals;
    Maintain humanitarian relations with help requests;
    Project the number of vaccinated individuals;
    Ensure data security.

Development of Pulmonary Ventilators using Internet of Things (IoT) for Combating Covid-19.

  • This project aims to develop a prototype of a Pulmonary Ventilator using IoT, with the goal of assisting in combating the symptoms of Covid-19.

    Benefits:
    Model a prototype of pulmonary ventilators using specific software;
    Develop a prototype for testing with specialized medical supervision;
    Develop a large-scale production process for pulmonary ventilators.

Smart Agriculture System

  • The smart agricultural system consists of hardware with embedded software utilizing artificial intelligence algorithms that classify plant and fruit diseases through digital image processing.

    Benefits:
    The project will benefit small farmers and provide alternative solutions for small properties in rural and urban areas.
    Development of smart agriculture and technologies involving FMM students.

Integrated Edge IIoT Platform for Hospitals, Agribusiness, Industry, and Cities.

  • This project consists of an integrated Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) platform (HW) and software (SW) for hospitals, agribusiness, industry, and cities.

    Benefits:
    Production of a low-cost product for IoT implementation in hospitals, residences, agriculture, and industry.

Boat Location System on Amazon Rivers using GPS/Satellite in Areas without Phone and Internet Coverage.

  • Develop a solution to the lack of signal coverage using information with artificial intelligence algorithms that classify data such as speed, acceleration, departure and arrival points, and travel history, to feed a knowledge base to be processed by AI and/or Fuzzy Logic algorithms.

    Benefits:
    Route definition in hard-to-access areas, assistance for small and large navigation, production, and elaboration of cartographic maps for the Armed Forces and commercial navigation.

Optimization using Genetic Algorithms for Stabilizing Soil-Cement Mixtures for Manufacturing Artifacts in Civil Construction

  • Evaluate the potential for reusing industrial waste: Granite and marble cutting waste (RCGM), red ceramic waste (RCV), carbide lime waste (RCC), and glass powder waste (RPV) in the manufacturing of monolithic plates based on soil-cement technology for constructing walls of low-income housing.

    Benefits:
    Construction of cheaper low-income housing, which can also be included in the Minha Casa, Minha Vida or Viver Melhor programs.

Self-Sustainable Hydrogen-based Electricity Generation

  • Develop an experimental prototype for generating electricity from hydrogen.

    Benefits:
    Plan, develop, and implement an experimental plant for electricity generation;
    Analyze the various aspects of the economic feasibility of hydrogen-based electricity to be presented to Aneel and the Hydrogen Research Center (NUPHI);
    Develop mathematical models to compare with other sources of electricity generation.

Professional Training and Certification Courses for Workforce Entry

Scientific and Technological Research on the Viability of New Photovoltaic Materials for Electricity Conversion

  • Research new materials and develop new technologies to enable innovation in solar panel manufacturing.

      Selection of regional materials with the potential for photovoltaic application;

      Analyze different materials and propose which technology to use.
    Research new materials and develop new technologies to enable innovation in solar panel manufacturing.

Controlled Access Project to Various Environments using Artificial Intelligence

  • Develop an intelligent facial recognition system to be used in fixed devices (computational controllers) and/or mobile devices (smartphones) to be applied in access control to restricted environments or authorization of vehicular electric connection.

    Benefits:
    Use of neural networks and decision trees;
    Filtering techniques based on Kalman Filter and Particle Filter;
    Data analysis techniques;
    Construction of event-driven architecture (EDA);
    Development of microservices;
    Possibility of using local or cloud services (private or hybrid).
